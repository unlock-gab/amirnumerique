## Official brand direction

Use the following brand colors as the core identity:

* `#8c0014` (deep brand red / RAL 3004)
* `#e30613` (bright brand red / RAL 3020)

The website should move to a **premium white / light theme** based on the real brand identity.

## New visual objective

Create a refined, premium, professional light theme that feels:

* elegant
* branded
* modern
* corporate
* high-end
* aligned with a real print/signage company identity

## Theme direction

Use:

* white / off-white main backgrounds
* soft light gray section backgrounds
* charcoal / near-black for headings
* muted gray for body text
* official brand reds for accents, highlights, CTAs, active states, key icons, and important UI moments

## Typography

Align the typography system with the brand direction:

* titles/headings should feel inspired by **Avant Garde ITC Bold**
* body text should feel inspired by **Montserrat Regular**
* maintain strong readability in both French and Arabic

If the exact title font is not available, choose the closest premium alternative with the same spirit.
